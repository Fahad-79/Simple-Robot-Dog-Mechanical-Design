# Simple Robot Dog Mechanical Design

## Project Overview
This project presents the initial mechanical design of a simple quadruped robot dog. The focus is on understanding the basic mechanical concepts required for a robot to stand and walk.

## Body Structure
- Rectangular aluminum chassis
- Length: 25 cm
- Width: 15 cm
- Height: 8 cm

## Leg Design
The robot has four legs. Each leg consists of:
- Upper Leg
- Lower Leg
- Foot

## Degrees of Freedom (DOF)
Each leg has:
- Hip Joint (1 DOF)
- Knee Joint (1 DOF)

Total DOF = 8

## Motors
- MG996R Servo Motors
- Total Motors: 8

## Joint Type
- Revolute Joint

## Stability
The battery and controller are mounted in the center of the body to keep the center of gravity balanced.

## Walking Method
Crawl Gait:
1. Front Left
2. Rear Right
3. Front Right
4. Rear Left

## Expected Mechanical Problems
- Leg slipping
- Motor overload
- Body vibration
- Joint wear
- Battery consumption
- Balance issues

## Conclusion
This project demonstrates the mechanical design principles of a simple quadruped robot dog suitable for educational purposes.
